The story

Welcome to MokeSell – The Marketplace for Everyone

MokeSell is a dynamic e-commerce platform founded by a group of passionate students from Ngee Ann Polytechnic. Our mission is to create a seamless and trustworthy space where anyone can buy and sell both new and second-hand goods with ease. Whether you're looking to score a great deal or declutter and earn extra cash, MokeSell connects buyers and sellers in a safe, convenient, and community-driven marketplace.

What We Do

MokeSell operates as a digital marketplace where individuals can list their pre-loved or brand-new items for sale. Our platform ensures a smooth transaction process, offering features such as secure payments, reliable listings, and an intuitive interface for browsing and purchasing goods. Sellers can reach a wide audience, while buyers can explore a diverse range of products at competitive prices.

How MokeSell Helps the Community

MokeSell goes beyond just being a marketplace—it fosters a culture of sustainability, affordability, and accessibility. By promoting the resale of second-hand goods, we contribute to reducing environmental waste and making quality items available to those who need them at budget-friendly prices. Additionally, we empower students, young entrepreneurs, and everyday individuals to turn unused items into extra income, helping to support financial independence and resourcefulness.

Join us at MokeSell and be part of a smarter, more sustainable way to buy and sell!
